R-12.2 In the merge-sort tree shown in Figures 12.2 through 12.4, some 
edges are drawn as arrows. What is the meaning of a downward arrow? How 
about an upward arrow?

Answer:
======

The downward arrow meaning that the input data are being divided until a 
lower fraction and the upward arrow indicates that the subsets are being 
merged into a larger subsets until turn up a only ordered data set.
